Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

At Fitworx Gym, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. We handle 50-100 customers daily and maintain strict data protection standards for all our members.

2. Information We Collect

Basic Personal Information: We collect essential personal details during membership registration at our cashier area.

Membership Status: Information about your membership type (Regular ₱950/Student ₱850) and renewal dates.

Facility Usage: Records of basketball court, Zumba session bookings.

Student Verification: Basic information to verify student status for discounted rates.

3. How We Use Your Information

To manage your gym membership and track renewal dates.

To handle facility reservations and prevent booking conflicts.

To process payments and maintain accurate financial records.

To communicate with you through our front desk staff via Messenger.

4. Information Sharing

Information is shared only with our staff (2 front desk/cashier and 3 maintenance personnel) for operational purposes.

Freelance personal trainers receive only necessary information to provide their services.

We do not share your information for marketing purposes beyond our Facebook advertising.

5. Data Security

We maintain strict control over physical records and documentation.

Access to member information is restricted to authorized front desk staff only.

Daily verification and secure storage of all member records.
