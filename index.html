Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gym

What are the gym's operating hours?
Fitworx Gym is open from 9:00 AM to 11:59 PM daily, providing flexible hours to accommodate your fitness schedule.
Do you offer personal training?
Yes, we have personal trainers but all the coaches in our gym are freelance only - you can message them if you want to.
Are there any age restrictions?
Members must be at least 16 years old. Individuals between 16-18 years require parental consent. We offer student memberships with special rates for those in school.
What facilities are included in my membership?
Your membership includes full access to our gym floor.
